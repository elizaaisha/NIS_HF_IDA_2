Outcomes in HFrEF vs HFpEF in Patients with Iron Deficiency Anemia

Author
Affiliation

Eliza Aisha

Dow University of Health Sciences

Preamble:

  • Data Source: Cross-sectional analysis of the National Inpatient Sample (NIS) from 2018 to 2020.

  • Patient Selection: Included all inpatient admissions with Iron deficiency anemia.

  • HF Categories:

    • Heart failure with reduced ejection fraction

    • Heart failure with preserved ejection fraction

  • ICD Codes:

    • HFrEF = I50.2 and I50.4
    • HFpEF = I50.3

  • Primary Outcomes:

    • In-hospital all-cause mortality

    • Total hospital length of stay (days)

    • Total charge, inflation adjusted to 2020 ($)

  • Software: All analyses were performed using R Version 4.4.1 (R Foundation for Statistical Computing, Vienna, Austria)
